High-In Mathematics

Several primary school mathematics scholars have designed a systematic mathematics curriculum. Under the guidance, students can develop their ability to think analytically and solve problems.

Features:

  1. Basic math training:
    • Training students’ basic calculation skills (straight and horizontal).
    • Raising student calculation speed.
  2. Problem solving strategy:
    • Teach the procedure of answering application problems.
    • Teach appropriate problem-solving strategies. (e.g. analysis, simplification, listing, illustration, etc.)
    • Provide a series of problem-solving exercises to train students’ thinking skills and the entire application process.
  3. Basic math training:
    • Inference exercises such as numerical/graphic logic to train students' logical and systemic thinking skills.
