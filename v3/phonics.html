High-In Phonics

This course is not intended to teach the International Phonetic Alphabet. Students will learn the pronunciation of each English alphabet in the words (for example, consonants, vowels; long vowels, short vowels, etc.). Through the classroom activities, students can read unlearnt vocabularies and sentences, more advanced, it is very useful for unseen dictation.

Features:

  • Improve the ability to read and spell in English;
  • In the absence of supplementary teaching materials (such as tapes, CD-ROMs, etc.), enhance students’ interest in and ability to read, write, and read in English;
  • In our 75-hour course, the content is often equivalent to a typical phonetic course of 2.5 to 3.5 years;
  • The students will teach parents phonics as “little teachers”;
  • Parents can see the students’ learning outcomes, such as show-time after class, classroom observation, and even larger shows (depending on the venue and student conditions);
  • A certificate can be issued at all stages of completion;
  • Interim admission is not allowed, but students who have learned phonics before could be allowed after individual assessments. (Can save time and money)
