Our Mission

Along their paths to knowledge students often experience difficulties. Therefore, their moods could swing like winds. Sometimes it is like "breeze" - mild, steady and calm. However, when students with poor academic ability encounter difficulties, the wind may transform into a "storm" - fierce, intense and non-directional.

Before the situation deteriorates, our centre will perform as the “lead-out man” to navigate through the storm. We will lead in front of the students to guide them through difficulties and break learning barriers. In other words, we must not evade but rather help students overcome wind resistance and face and deal with their difficulties in learning. We want students to maintain the overall learning rhythm in order to save the best for the sprint.

We will let the students know that the academic problems encountered in the school are not dead ends. We will be there to solve with them. We will enhance students' self-esteem by achieving better academic performance in weaker subjects. In addition, we will pay attention to the student’s learning trajectory and turn “storm” into a tailwind to move on.

A. For IGCSE/GCSE/GCE/IB Regular Courses (Individual/Group)
  1. Individuals/group students must notify the Centres of leave by oral or written notice two working days prior to the commencement of class. Successful application of leave can be handled with one of the following two options:
    1. The class can be re-arranged to another date within that month agreed by both the tutor and the student(s).
    2. If re-arrangement of class is not applicable, the fees of this session will be carried forward to the next month. This arrangement will be limited to once a month.
  2. If students in the same group are not all on leave, point (a)(i) is not applicable for the absentee(s). Only point (a)(ii) will be applicable.
B. For Co-organized Courses

Should a student need to be absent from a class, the tuition fee for that session will not be refund. No re-arrangement of class will be made.

If classes are cancelled due to bad weather, they will be re-arranged or postponed.
